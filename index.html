About Us

Steam Team is a mobile clothing care unit located in New York City. We come to our clients to iron, press, steam clean, and fold clothing, per clients' requests. We help our clients dress to impress and keep their clothing looking presentable without taking time out of their busy schedules. We want to reach clients nationwide with opportunities to be impeccably dressed while providing continual customer quality care.
